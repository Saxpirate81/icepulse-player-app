## üí™ Upper Body Exercises (8 exercises)

### 1. Push-Ups
- **Reps:** 15 | **Sets:** 3
- Build chest, shoulder, and tricep strength
- Variations: Knee push-ups (beginner), Diamond (advanced)

### 2. Pike Push-Ups
- **Reps:** 12 | **Sets:** 3
- Strengthen shoulders for shooting power
- Intermediate difficulty

### 3. Diamond Push-Ups
- **Reps:** 10 | **Sets:** 3
- Target triceps and inner chest
- Advanced variation

### 4. Plank Shoulder Taps
- **Reps:** 20 | **Sets:** 3
- Core stability and shoulder strength
- Intermediate difficulty

### 5. Arm Circles
- **Reps:** 30 | **Sets:** 2
- Warm up and strengthen shoulders
- Beginner friendly

### 6. Wall Angels
- **Reps:** 15 | **Sets:** 3
- Improve shoulder mobility and posture
- Beginner friendly

### 7. Superman Holds
- **Timer:** 30 seconds | **Sets:** 3
- Strengthen lower back and improve posture
- Isometric hold exercise

### 8. Burpees
- **Reps:** 10 | **Sets:** 3
- Full-body explosive movement
- Advanced cardio + strength

## üèÉ Cardio & Conditioning (8 exercises)

### 1. High Knees
- **Work:** 30s | **Rest:** 30s | **Rounds:** 6
- Improve cardiovascular fitness and leg speed
- Run in place with high knees

### 2. Mountain Climbers
- **Work:** 30s | **Rest:** 30s | **Rounds:** 5
- Full-body cardio and core strength
- Plank position alternating knees

### 3. Jumping Jacks
- **Work:** 30s | **Rest:** 30s | **Rounds:** 6
- Classic cardio for endurance
- Full-body movement

### 4. Butt Kicks
- **Work:** 30s | **Rest:** 30s | **Rounds:** 6
- Improve hamstring flexibility and cardio
- Run in place kicking heels up

### 5. Skaters (Lateral Jumps)
- **Work:** 30s | **Rest:** 30s | **Rounds:** 6
- Hockey-specific lateral power and agility
- Mimics skating motion

### 6. Burpees
- **Work:** 20s | **Rest:** 40s | **Rounds:** 5
- Full-body explosive cardio
- High intensity

### 7. Bike Interval Training
- **Work:** 30s | **Rest:** 60s | **Rounds:** 6
- Cardiovascular endurance (if stationary bike available)
- Note: Can substitute with High Knees if no bike

### 8. Sprint Intervals (In Place)
- **Work:** 20s | **Rest:** 40s | **Rounds:** 6
- Build speed and anaerobic capacity
- Maximum effort running in place

## ‚ö° Agility & Speed (4 exercises)

### 1. Ladder Drills (Imaginary)
- **Reps:** 5 | **Sets:** 3
- Improve foot speed and coordination
- Use tape on floor or imaginary ladder

### 2. Lateral Shuffles
- **Reps:** 20 | **Sets:** 3
- Improve lateral movement for defensive play
- Stay low, shuffle side to side

### 3. Carioca (Grapevine)
- **Reps:** 10 | **Sets:** 3
- Improve hip mobility and coordination
- Cross legs front and back while moving sideways

### 4. Quick Feet (In Place)
- **Timer:** 30 seconds | **Sets:** 3
- Develop fast twitch muscles for quick starts
- Rapidly tap feet in place
